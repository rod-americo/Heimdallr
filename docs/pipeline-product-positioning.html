Pipeline Product Positioning

Positioning of Heimdallr projects across the radiology pipeline: before report, during report, and after report.

Product Line

This page consolidates the product line by workflow stage and delivered value.

Before Report

  • Ingestion, prefetch, routing, privacy controls, and queue readiness.
  • Goal: reduce waiting and context-friction before first image open.
  • Primary product axis: throughput reliability and operational control.

During Report

  • Assistive triage, pre-analysis, quantification, and structured drafting support.
  • Goal: improve speed-to-read and consistency without replacing clinical judgment.
  • Primary product axis: silent workflow acceleration at read time.

After Report

  • Follow-up extraction, navigation, closure tracking, and longitudinal monitoring.
  • Goal: convert recommendations into auditable clinical action.
  • Primary product axis: continuity of care and closure outcomes.

Strategic Read

  • Near term: lock in the before + during blocks to sustain measurable throughput and TAT improvements.
  • Mid term: promote operational intelligence modules once workflow telemetry is stable and auditable.
  • Long term: scale post-report navigation and closure modules to capture continuity and financial value.
